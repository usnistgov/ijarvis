OPTIMADE

The Open Databases Integration for Materials Design (OPTIMADE)[https://www.optimade.org/] consortium offers a common application programming interface (API) to make materials databases accessible and interoperable. JARVIS-OPTIMADE is designed to provide JARVIS data in REST-API format following OPTIMADE protocols.
